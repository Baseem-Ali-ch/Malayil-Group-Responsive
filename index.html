Welcome to Malayil Food Park

Established in the year 1994, Malayil food park is successfully catering the superior quality of food products like rice, spices, flours, pickles, and different types of pulses, etc... to our clients. These products are widely marketed in Kerala, nearby states and exporting to other countries. We are flourishing day by day, under the able veneration of C H Mohammed Gaddafi. He believes in hard work and consistency and advocates that one should always make the best efforts.without losing their nutrition, taste, texture, and aroma.
